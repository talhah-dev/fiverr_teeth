FAQ

Quick questions, clear answers

Here are a few common questions about our service and what to expect.

Is Dentist Near Me free?
Yes—Dentist Near Me is a free service that helps patients connect with local dental professionals.
Are the dentists part of Dentist Near Me?
Dental professionals are independent. It’s the patient’s responsibility to verify licensing and insurance.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
Timing depends on local availability and your needs. Our process is built to be fast and clear so you can schedule as soon as possible.
Is this medical advice?
No. This site provides referral assistance. Your dentist will diagnose and recommend the right treatment plan for your situation.
