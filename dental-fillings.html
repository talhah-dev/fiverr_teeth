WHAT ARE FILLINGS?

A simple treatment that saves the tooth

A dental filling removes decayed tooth structure and seals the area to prevent future damage. It restores the tooth’s shape, strength, and bite.

Stops cavities
Removes decay and seals the tooth.
Protects enamel
Helps prevent future sensitivity and damage.
Restores appearance
Tooth-colored options can blend naturally.
Common signs you may need a filling
If you notice any of these, schedule an evaluation.
Sensitivity
Pain with hot, cold, or sweet foods.
Visible spots
Dark areas or holes on the tooth surface.
Pain when chewing
Discomfort biting or pressure sensitivity.
Rough edges
Chips or worn areas that catch your tongue.
A dentist will confirm the best treatment option and check if a filling is appropriate for your tooth.
