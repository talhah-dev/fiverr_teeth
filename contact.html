FAQ

Questions? We’ve got answers

Here are some of the most common questions about our referral service and what to expect next.

Is this service free?
Yes. Dentist Near Me is a free referral service that helps connect patients with local dental professionals.
Are dentists part of Dentist Near Me?
Dental professionals are independent. This site does not warrant or guarantee any work performed. Patients should verify licensing and insurance.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
Availability varies by location and service type. We aim to connect you as quickly as possible so you can schedule with a local provider.
What information should I provide?
Share the type of dentist or service you need, your general location, and any timing preferences. The more details you provide, the better the match.
Is this medical or dental advice?
No. We provide referral assistance only. Your dental professional will evaluate your situation and recommend the right treatment.
Do you accept insurance?
Insurance acceptance depends on the individual provider. Ask the dental office directly about coverage, pricing, and payment options.
