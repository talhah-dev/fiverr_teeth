FAQ

Quick answers about extractions

Every case is different, but these are common questions people ask before an extraction appointment.

Is a tooth extraction painful?
Dentists use local anesthesia to numb the area. You may feel pressure, but sharp pain is typically minimized. Aftercare instructions help manage soreness.
How long does recovery take?
Many people feel better within a few days. Healing time can vary based on the tooth, difficulty, and your health. Follow your dentist’s guidance closely.
What should I avoid after an extraction?
Your dentist may recommend avoiding hard foods, smoking, or intense rinsing early on. Stick to soft foods and keep the area clean as instructed.
Do I need to replace the tooth?
It depends on the tooth and your bite. Some extractions may require a replacement option to help maintain alignment and function.
