FAQ

Common questions about cleanings

Here are quick answers about visit frequency, comfort, and what to do if you have sensitivity.

How often should I get a cleaning?
Many people schedule routine cleanings regularly. Your dentist may recommend a different schedule based on gum health and buildup.
Are cleanings painful?
Cleanings are typically comfortable, but sensitivity can happen. Tell your dentist so they can adjust technique and improve comfort.
Will a cleaning whiten my teeth?
Cleanings remove surface stains and buildup, which can make teeth look brighter. Whitening treatments are different and can be discussed separately.
What if I have bleeding gums?
Bleeding can happen with inflammation or irritation. A dentist can evaluate your gums and recommend next steps for better gum health.
